Oto Ai

An AI enabled digital otoscope which classifies ear pathology at 90% accuracy. 2020 Aug - 2022 May

This device helps primary care physician identify pathology and capture images to send to a specialist. This project includes an easy to use button and encasing, a desktop app for Mac and PC which allows physicians to generate reports in a format that is compatible with EMRs and a transfer learning based CNN to classify the imagees in seconds. This project won the 2021 Senior Design Award for Technology and innovation.
